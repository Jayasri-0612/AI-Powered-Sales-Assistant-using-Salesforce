## 📌 **Problem Statement**

**Sales and service teams** often rely on **manual processes** to manage customer data, track service requests, and monitor sales opportunities. This leads to **delays, data inconsistencies, and reduced productivity**.

Additionally, lack of **real-time insights** and **automation** makes it difficult for teams to **respond quickly to customer needs** and **prioritize critical cases effectively**.


## 🎯 **Objective**

The objective of this project is to **design and implement a Salesforce-based Smart Service and Sales Management System** to improve overall business efficiency.

The system focuses on **automating workflows**, **enhancing data accuracy**, and providing **real-time insights** to support better decision-making.

Additionally, it aims to **streamline sales and service operations**, **reduce manual effort**, and **increase productivity** using **Salesforce automation, reports, dashboards, and AI-driven features**.
