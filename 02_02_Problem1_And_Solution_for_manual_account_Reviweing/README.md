## 📌 **Problem 1: Manual Account Analysis**

Sales representatives spend a significant amount of time manually reviewing account details, recent activities, and opportunities in the CRM. This process is time-consuming, inefficient, and may lead to missed insights and delayed decision-making.

---

## 💡 **Solution**

To address this issue, an **AI-Powered Sales Assistant** was developed using Salesforce Agentforce.

A chatbot interface is integrated into the Salesforce UI, allowing users to enter prompts like **“Show account summary”**. The system automatically analyzes CRM data and generates a **concise and meaningful summary** of the account.

This reduces manual effort, saves time, and helps sales representatives make faster and more informed decisions.
